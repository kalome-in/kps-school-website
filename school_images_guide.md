## 💡 Professional Photography Tips for KPS

To ensure the school website maintains its high-quality, premium aesthetic:

1. **Maximize Natural Light**: Shoot outdoor campus pictures during the "golden hours" (shortly after sunrise or before sunset) to get warm, soft lighting. For indoor classrooms, turn on all lights and open windows to avoid dark, grainy shots.
2. **Action & Candid over Posed**: Show children *in action* (writing on a whiteboard, looking through a microscope, laughing with classmates) rather than staring stiffly at the camera.
3. **High Resolution**: Take photos using a dedicated DSLR camera or a modern smartphone set to the highest resolution (12MP or more).
4. **Clean Backgrounds**: Before taking a photo of a classroom or office, ensure clutter, waste bins, or stray bags are moved out of frame.
5. **Horizontal Orientation**: Keep the camera horizontal (landscape mode) for all photos except specific portrait slots. This ensures they fit perfectly into the 16:9 or 3:2 grids.
